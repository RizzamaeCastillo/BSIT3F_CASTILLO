University Library Online Reservation

University Library Online Reservation is a digital system designed to streamline the process of reserving library resources such as books, study rooms, or computer workstations. It allows students, faculty, and staff to browse available materials, check schedules, and make reservations conveniently from their devices. This system enhances library efficiency, reduces manual work, and ensures fair access to resources by managing bookings and availability in real time
